Wonderful Indonesia

17,000 islands . 300 ethnicities.

From majestic mountains to glorious temples, discover the most beautiful places in the country. With an incredibly lively underwater scene, Indonesia is home to more than 530 species of coral, 700 species of mollusk and 1,300 types of fish.

Indonesia consists of thousands of distinct native ethnic and hundreds of linguistic groups, with Javanese being the largest. A shared identity has developed with the motto "Bhinneka Tunggal Ika" ("Unity in Diversity" literally, "many, yet one"), defined by a national language, cultural diversity, religious pluralism within a Muslim-majority population

Batu

Batu (means "rock" in Indonesian), officially the City of Batu, a landlocked city in the East Java Province of Indonesia. It is about 20 km to the northwest of Malang. Batu is well known for its tourism sites, especially for family.
